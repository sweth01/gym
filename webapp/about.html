About FitAnywhere

FitAnywhere was founded in 2023 by fitness industry veterans who believed that the traditional health club model wasn’t meeting the needs of most people. They started FitAnywhere to give customers more of what they really want out of a fitness membership. The result is a simple fitness club concept – an extremely affordable, family-friendly facility that offers state-of-the-art cardio, strength, and free weight equipment.

Our knowledgeable, friendly staff, and local management combine to make FitAnywhere the club for families who want a healthy lifestyle without the hefty cost. Come check us out!
